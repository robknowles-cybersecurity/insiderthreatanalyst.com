KSA → NICE Crosswalk v0.1 · draft

A working draft mapping the framework's 144 Knowledge, Skill, Ability, and Task statements to the NICE Workforce Framework for Cybersecurity (NIST SP 800-181r1). The NICE Work Role and TKS columns are intentionally blank — this scaffold captures the KSA rows and the target vocabulary; the mapping itself is pending.

Status — mapping in progress. This is a structural scaffold (PE-125). The 144 KSA rows below are drawn from the framework's KSA catalogue (framework.html). The NICE Work Role(s) and NICE TKS columns are unpopulated pending a dedicated mapping pass. NICE reference: NICE Framework Components v2.2.0 (released 2026-04-28), NIST SP 800-181r1 — Work Role Categories, Work Roles, Competency Areas, and Task / Knowledge / Skill (TKS) statements. The machine-readable v2.2.0 Work Role and TKS vocabulary is to be ingested from the NIST CPRT export on the mapping pass; it was not retrievable via automated fetch at scaffold time.
