New here?

If you're breaking into insider threat or trying to figure out what the field even looks like, start with Start Here. It's a plain-English guide to the roles, the path in, and what to expect.

What is this?

A practitioner-built competency model for the insider threat field. It maps the knowledge, skills, and abilities an analyst needs at every career level — from junior to senior principal — across nine concentrations. It also curates the tools, certifications, training, program design resources, and emerging threat intelligence that practitioners actually use.

Built by an insider threat analyst, informed by the FS-ISAC Insider Threat Working Group, presented at the CMU SEI OSIT Working Group. Open to everyone.

How do I use the framework explorer?

Go to the Framework Explorer. Pick a concentration (e.g., threat hunter, case officer, technical specialist) and a career level. The page filters KSAs, certifications, tools, and training to that specific role. You can export a PDF or generate a job description directly from the explorer.

Can I contribute?

Yes. Suggest a resource, correction, KSA refinement, or anything else. Contributions are credited on the Contributors page. See Site Policy for how changes are evaluated.
