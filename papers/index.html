Detecting IP-KVM Devices in the Enterprise
Rob Knowles · April 2026 · 23 pages CC BY 4.0
Detection methodology for IP-KVM hardware in enterprise environments. IP-KVM devices convert USB to network and expose remote keyboard, video, and mouse control over IP — used legitimately for out-of-band sysadmin work and adversarially for unauthorized remote presence, including in the DPRK IT-worker pattern and broader work-avoidance schemes. The paper walks through signal sources, correlation patterns, and triage workflows analysts can apply against existing telemetry without specialized tooling.
