About

Who built this, and why.

Rob Knowles

Hi, I'm Rob Knowles. I'm a Principal Insider Threat Analyst at a major US financial institution, where I spend my days thinking about how organizations can better understand and manage the risks that come from the inside. I hold a CISSP and GCITP, an M.S. in Cybersecurity from George Mason University, and I currently chair the FS-ISAC Insider Threat Working Group Steering Committee.

I built this site because the insider threat field has too many good frameworks that don't talk to each other. Practitioners end up doing their own crosswalks on whiteboards and spreadsheets, reinventing the same mapping work over and over. The goal here is to do that work once, in the open, so everyone can build on it instead of rebuilding it. Everything on this site is published under CC BY 4.0 — use it, adapt it, make it better.

If you want to get in touch, find me on LinkedIn.
